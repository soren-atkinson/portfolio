About Pokémon Team Builder

Hi, I'm Soren Atkinson, and I created the Pokémon Team Builder as part of a college project. As a passionate Pokémon fan and aspiring developer, I wanted to combine my love for the series with my web development skills to build something useful for fellow fans.

This project was an opportunity to dive deep into web development concepts, including APIs, responsive design, and user interactivity. Using PokéAPI as the backbone for Pokémon data, I was able to create a tool that generates teams based on customizable rules and preferences.

The process taught me a lot about problem-solving, coding best practices, and the joy of creating something others can enjoy. I hope you find the Pokémon Team Builder helpful and fun to use!

If you have feedback or want to connect, feel free to reach out. Thanks for visiting!
